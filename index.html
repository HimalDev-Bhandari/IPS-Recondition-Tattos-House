About Us

Founded by Mr. Shyamu Bhandari, blending mechanical expertise with artistic tattoo craftsmanship

Your Trusted Partner in Surkhet

IPS Recondition and Tattoos House was founded by Mr. Shyamu Bhandari to create a unique space where mechanical craftsmanship meets artistic expression. Located in the heart of Birendranagar, Surkhet, we've become a cultural hub for bike enthusiasts and tattoo lovers alike.

We pride ourselves on honest, affordable, and high-quality services for customers across Surkhet and neighboring areas. Customer satisfaction is our top priority, and we strive to deliver reliable and friendly service at every step.

Our journey began with a passion for transforming two-wheelers and a love for tattoo artistry. Today, we stand as the only establishment in Surkhet that offers premium bike services alongside custom tattoo designs.
